Dr. ARTH SHAH M.S., M.Ch, (Cosmetic and Plastic surgeon) Fellow Hair Transplant Surgery, Fellow advanced cosmetic surgery, Fellow LASER and cosmetic dermatology is a Board Certified Internationally qualified, highly experienced Cosmetic and Plastic surgeon practicing in Ahmedabad. Civil Hospital being the largest hospital of Asia Dr. shah has wide exposure of all the fields of plastic and cosmetic surgery.

1) TATA Memorial Hospital, Mumbai
